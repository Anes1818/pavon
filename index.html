Keep them alive +7 days

Flower care, the simple way.

✂️
Cut at an angle

Trim 2cm off each stem at 45° under running water. Do it again every two days — stems drink from the cut.

💧
Fresh, cool water

Change the water every 1–2 days and remove any leaf that touches it. Bacteria is the real bouquet killer.

🌤️
Cool spot, no fruit

Keep away from direct sun, radiators and fruit bowls — ripening fruit releases gas that ages flowers fast.
